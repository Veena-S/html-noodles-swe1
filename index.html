Noodles

Noodles are usually cooked in boiling water, sometimes with cooking oil or salt added.
They are also often pan-fried or deep-fried. Noodle dishes can include a sauce or noodles can be put into soup.
The material composition and geocultural origin is specific to each type of a wide variety of noodles.
Noodles are a staple food in many cultures

Different types of noodles are available.

  • Onion based Noodles
  • Chicken Noodles
